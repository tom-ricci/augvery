Genderfluid is a gender identity which refers to a gender which varies over time. A genderfluid person's identity may change constantly, so it's always best to ask what they are in that moment. However, I'm Demifluid, which is a form of genderfluidity in which my gender changes between Agender, Demiboy, Demigirl, and Demibigender, meaning no matter how I identify, I'll always use they/cae. I also accept he/they while I'm a Demiboy, fey/they/she when I'm a Demigirl, and he/fey/they/cae/she while I'm Demibigender.
